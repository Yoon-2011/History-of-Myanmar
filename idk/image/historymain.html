The Kingdom Era

Myanmar's kingdom era was marked by the rise and fall of powerful dynasties, beginning with the unified Pagan Kingdom (1044-1287) under King Anawrahta, which established a cultural and political foundation for the nation.The final major dynasty, the Konbaung Dynasty, ruled until the British conquest in 1885, which ended the monarchy.


The Colonial era

The Myanmar colonial era, spanning from 1824 to 1948, was a period of British rule that began with the Anglo-Burmese Wars and ended with independence. During this time, Britain annexed Burma, first making it a province of British India before making it a separate colony in 1937.
